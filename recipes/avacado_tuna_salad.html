Avocado and Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna in water, drained
  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt for a healthier option)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: A squeeze of lemon juice, chopped cilantro, or parsley for garnish

Process

  1. Scoop the flesh of the avocado into a bowl, leaving the shell intact for serving.
  2. Add the drained tuna to the bowl with the avocado.
  3. Add mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix well. Optionally, add a squeeze of lemon juice or some herbs.
  5. Scoop the mixture back into the empty avocado shells for a neat presentation or serve as is.
