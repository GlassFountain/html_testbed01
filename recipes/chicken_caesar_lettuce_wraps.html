Chicken Caesar Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • Cooked chicken breast, sliced or shredded (can be leftovers or pre-cooked)
  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese, croutons for those not strictly low-carb

Process

  1. Lay out the Romaine lettuce leaves on a plate.
  2. Place the cooked chicken slices or shreds on the lettuce.
  3. Drizzle Caesar dressing over the chicken.
  4. Optionally, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese or add a couple of croutons if you're not strictly low-carb.
  5. Fold the lettuce over the toppings and enjoy as a wrap.
