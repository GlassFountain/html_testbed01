Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized zucchinis
  • 1/4 cup pesto sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Process

  1. Using a spiralizer or a grater, turn the zucchinis into noodles.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the zucchini noodles and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  4. Add the pesto sauce to the zucchini noodles and mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Optionally, garnish with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
